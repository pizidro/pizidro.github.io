About

Finance meets curiosity

I'm an Investment Operations Associate at Cambridge Associates, where I support institutional clients with portfolio data management across traditional and alternative asset classes. My work sits at the intersection of rigorous data analysis and client-facing problem solving.

I hold a Master's in International Economics and Finance from Johns Hopkins University (SAIS) and a BA in Political Economy from Williams College — a combination that shapes how I approach markets: always with one eye on the macro.

When I'm not in the data, I'm thinking about monetary policy, exchange rates, cross-border trade — or planning the next trip. I'm a native Portuguese and English speaker, and hold intermediate Spanish and French.
