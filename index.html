Popular Travel Tips

  • Traveling makes you awesome in the sense that it combats narrow-mindedness. It also makes you more confident, mode adaptable, smarter and happier.

  • Purchasing travel insurance is one of the most important things you'll need when traveling. You may purchase insurance with any of the hundreds of insurance companies that exist out there -- I personally recommend World Nomads.

  • Looking for a cheap flight? Aren't we all! Head over to Google Flights and type in where you're flying to. Keep in mind that being flexible with your destinations and joining mailing lists may be beneficial as well. Patience is key when looking for discounted flights.
